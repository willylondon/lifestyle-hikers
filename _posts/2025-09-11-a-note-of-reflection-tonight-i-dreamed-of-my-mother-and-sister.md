# A Note of Reflection
Tonight I dreamed of my mother and sister.


![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*WbcFK5_9PeAr8aTctUj9gQ.png)A Note of Reflection
Tonight I dreamed of my mother and sister.
My mother was releasing a bird from the house —
a sign of freedom, of peace, of letting go.
My sister was there, unseen but present,
as if resting in another room, close but beyond my sight.
I take this dream as a reminder:
that love does not end with absence,
that release is not forgetting, but freeing,
and that those I’ve lost still walk with me,
even if softly, even if unseen.
May their spirits be at peace.
May my heart find comfort in their nearness.
And may I carry their love forward,
like wings that lift me when I need strength.
