Last Saturday, 28 members of Lifestyle Hikers tackled the iconic **Blue Mountain Peak Trail** — and what a morning it was!

## The Journey

We gathered at Whitfield Hall at 2:00 AM, headlamps blazing and thermoses full of Blue Mountain coffee (what else?). The trail was cool and misty, exactly the kind of conditions you want for this climb.

The group ranged from first-timers to veterans who've done the peak over a dozen times. That's what makes Lifestyle Hikers special — **every hike is a mix of experience levels**, and the seasoned ones always look out for the newcomers.

## The Sunrise

At 5:47 AM, the sun crept over the eastern ridge and painted the sky in gold and pink. From 7,402 feet, you can see all the way to Cuba on a clear day. This morning, the clouds sat below us like a white ocean — absolutely magical.

## Trail Tips

If you're planning to do Blue Mountain Peak, here's what we learned:

- **Start early** — 2 AM is ideal for a sunrise arrival
- **Dress in layers** — it's genuinely cold at the top (sometimes below 10°C)
- **Bring a headlamp** with extra batteries
- **Pack snacks** — you'll be hiking for 6-8 hours
- **Wear proper hiking boots** — the trail is rocky in sections
- **Stay hydrated** — carry at least 2 litres of water
