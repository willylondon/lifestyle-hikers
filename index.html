Our Story

More Than a Hiking Group

Lifestyle Hikers started as a small group of friends who shared a passion for the outdoors. What began as weekend walks quickly grew into a vibrant community of adventurers spanning all ages and skill levels.

We believe that the best trails are the ones you share with others. Whether you're a seasoned thru-hiker or lacing up boots for the first time, you'll find your place here.
