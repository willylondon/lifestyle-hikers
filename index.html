Our Story

Born in the Blue Mountains

Lifestyle Hikers was born from a love for Jamaica's incredible natural beauty. What started as a small group of friends exploring trails in the Blue Mountains has grown into one of the island's most vibrant outdoor communities.

From the misty peaks of Portland to the river gorges of St. Elizabeth, we explore every corner of Jamaica on foot. Whether yuh a seasoned trail runner or just looking fi get outdoors for the first time — you belong here.
